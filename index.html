Leon Kwasi Kuntuo-Asare is a published and spoken-word poet, essayist, short-story writer, publisher, historian, podcaster, blogger, activist, activist-athlete (his term for people who compete and rise money in charity sporting events),coder,investor, entrepreneur, martial artist, and photographer. He has written and published several books including the critically-acclaimed America:Divided We Stand?! series of political and social poetry, and the activist manifesto, The Revolution Wii Be Downloaded!: A Manifesto: Fighting a Peaceful Revolution with Tools From The Tech Revolution. Kuntuo-Asare, currently lives in San Francisco, California with his family.

An avid seeker of knowledge, Kuntuo-Asare has completed several educatiom programs at City College of San Francisco, on a diverse subjects including International Business, Administration of Justice, Creative Writing, just to name a few...
